FAQ

Does it work with Israeli stocks (TASE)? ▼

Yes. TradeIQ fully supports TASE — just add .TA suffix (e.g., TEVA.TA). The trade calculator includes Israeli tax calculation (25% capital gains + 3% sur-tax above ₪698,280).

How is this different from TradingView? ▼

TradingView is great for charting but has no trade calculator, no social sentiment aggregation, and no TASE support. TradeIQ is a decision tool — it tells you whether to enter a specific trade and at what size.

What data sources do you use? ▼

News: Marketaux + Finnhub. Social: StockTwits + Reddit. Market data: Yahoo Finance (real-time). All sources are aggregated and scored automatically.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes. Cancel from your account settings with one click. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
